Mentoring

Looking for advice on which colleges to apply to? Having some trouble taking standardized tests? Need a fresh set of eyes to critique an essay? Want a fellow student’s help in drafting a course plan? Our mentoring program aims to connect high school students with college student mentors who will assist them in aspects such as the college application process, choosing courses, and resume building. All mentoring is free and hours are flexible to your schedule!
